About Me

Hi peeps!

Name given is Nur Rifqah Syazwina Binti Ruzaidi and you can call me Rif. I was born at Hospital Tuanku Ja’afar, Seremban (HTJS) on morning of December 9, 2000. I am proudly a Muslim Malaysian girl. I am single as I have bunch of life goals to achieve as a college student. I am the second child of five siblings and the only daughter of Ruzaidi Bin Deraman and Salmiah Binti Abdul Manap. My family and I currently lives in Seremban, Negeri Sembilan.

I do writing and reading in my free time. I am good at multitasking my work and have patience which I can endure a long wait calmly with annoying problems without frustration. I am also highly organized. I always take notes and I like to keep a clean workspace and create a logical filing method so I am always able to find what I need. Inter alia, I am a people-person. I love meeting new people and learning about their lives and their backgrounds. I can always find common ground with strangers, and I like making people feel comfortable in my presence.

About My Family

I belong to a middle class family. There are seven members in my family. They are my parents, my elder brother, three of my younger brothers and I.

My father is the head of the family. He enjoys a commanding position. His decision is final in family matters. Sometimes, nobody can dare to go against him as he still listens to our opinions and perspectives. Everybody respects him. He is the guardian of the family. He is a cool and considerate man. He is a technician.

My mother is a nurse instructor or to be in general, she is a lecturer. She is a postgraduate in Midwifery. She is cold-hearted but she is a caring woman. She takes great care of us. She does not care for her comfort for us. She looks after our both grandparents. She helps the poor and the needy. She is religious and God-fearing.

Our family has been known for discipline and values. We give great importance to values and morals in life. Since our early childhood we are taught to respect the elders and love the children. Our family is like a heaven. There is peace, prosperity, love and care. The younger’s have regard and respect for the elders, while the elders shower them with their love and affection.
